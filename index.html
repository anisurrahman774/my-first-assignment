Innovation

The statistical confidence interval for the ranking of Bangladesh in the GII 2022 is between ranks 93 and 110. Bangladesh performs better in innovation outputs than innovation inputs in 2022. This year Bangladesh ranks 112th in innovation inputs, higher than both 2021 and 2020.

Innovation

The Global Innovation Index (GII) ranks world economies according to their innovation capabilities. Consisting of roughly 80 indicators, grouped into innovation inputs and outputs, the GII aims to capture the multi-dimensional facets of innovation.
